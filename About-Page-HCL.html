About the Judiciary

High Court of Judicature at Lucknow

Historical Evolution

High Courts are the highest judicial bodies at the state level in India, functioning as the second-highest authority below the Supreme Court

There are currently 25 High Courts, established under the Constitution to handle appellate jurisdiction, writ petitions (Articles 226/227), and specific original civil/criminal matters.

Our Jurisdiction

The High Court has jurisdiction over the entire state of Uttar Pradesh, with its principal seat at Allahabad and a permanent bench at Lucknow.

Total Strength: Largest in India with a sanctioned strength of 160 Hon'ble Judges.

Key Milestones
