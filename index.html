Brajbhan Singh 'Bhanu'

General Secetory

Biography

Shri Brajbhan Singh 'Bhanu' is a distinguished legal luminary with over decades of experience in the High Court of Judicature at Lucknow. Known for his profound understanding of constitutional law and unwavering commitment to justice.

As the General Secetory, he has pioneered several digital initiatives to streamline case management and ensure transparency in judicial proceedings. His leadership continues to inspire the legal fraternity of Uttar Pradesh.

Key Achievements
  • Senior Advocate, High Court of Lucknow
  • Member of State Legal Services Authority
